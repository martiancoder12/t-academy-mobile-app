TT Academy · companion app · end to end

The complete app, one board

Thirty-five screens and a home-screen widget set covering the whole product — first run through exam booking, an in-app flashcards habit, plus the tutor’s half of the loop. The five-release roadmap is the app’s skeleton: the student always knows where they are (release + hours banked), what today contributes (every task logs a telemetry line), and what evidence the next gate still needs. Green / amber / red verdict tones are an intentional addition to the monochrome system; verdicts never rely on color alone. Four tabs: home · programs · flashcards · profile — the roadmap opens from the cadence strip on home, and programs is the website, mobile-optimized, in a webview.
